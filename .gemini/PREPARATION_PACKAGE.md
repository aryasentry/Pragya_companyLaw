## üö® **Emergency Quick Reference**

**If system crashes during demo:**
1. Stay calm - don't panic
2. Use backup screenshots/recording
3. Say: "Let me show you what it looks like..."
4. Continue with confidence

**If MD asks unexpected question:**
1. Pause - don't rush to answer
2. Check QUICK_REFERENCE.md Q&A section
3. If not covered, say: "Great question. Let me get you detailed information after the meeting."
4. Write it down - follow up later

**If running out of time:**
1. Skip to key demos (Definition + Business query)
2. Summarize other features verbally
3. Focus on business value and ROI
4. End with clear ask and next steps
